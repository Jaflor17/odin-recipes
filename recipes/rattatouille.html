Rattatouille

Straight from that Disney Movie

Honestly, if it weren’t for that Disney movie about the rat, I doubt my kids would have been so eager to try my ratatouille recipe.

But once they did, they were delighted and now it’s a dish I love to make when I feel like we should have something meatless. Made with a bounty of fresh, colorful vegetables, you’ll love the garden-fresh flavors in this tasty meal!

Ingredients

  • Eggplant
  • Zucchini
  • Roma Tomatoes
  • Crushed Tomoatoes
  • Carrot
  • Garlic
  • Cloves
  • Onions
  • Dried Herbs
  • Olive Oil

The Anyone can Cook Steps

  1. You’ll preheat the oven and meanwhile cook the onion, garlic, and carrot until it’s tender on the stove top.
  2. Add the crushed tomatoes and seasonings and simmer for about 15 minutes
  3. Taste at this point to see if the seasoning is balanced, and once the sauce is thick, pour it into your baking dish.
  4. Now you’ll arrange the slices of vegetables in a pretty circular pattern and brush with olive oil. Cover it and bake until done!
